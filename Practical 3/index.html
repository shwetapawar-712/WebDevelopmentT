This Institute, commonly referred as GCOEN, was started functioning in 2016 with 5 UG & 1 PG Engineering Programmes under the administrative control of Higher and Technical Education, Govt. of Maharashtra. The Institute is affiliated to RTM Nagpur University Nagpur. Within a short span of time, GCOEN has established its own identity not only in the Vidarbha region but also in the Maharashtra State. The students of high caliber are the backbones of the Institute.

Our Visions:
